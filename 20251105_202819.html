If you’ve ever dreamed of creating a killer app but don’t know how to code, Bolt.diy is your new best friend. It’s an open-source, AI-powered platform that helps you develop full-stack applications just by typing in prompts. No more wrangling with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, or back-end headaches. Think of it as ChatGPT meets app development — with results that are actually usable.
But here’s the best part: Bolt.diy is totally free and beginner-friendly. You don’t need a tech degree or years of experience. Just your ideas, a little curiosity, and maybe a strong cup of coffee.
5 Ways to Make Money with Bolt.diy:
- Freelance App Development: Head over to platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, or Toptal. You’ll find tons of clients looking for simple app solutions. Use Bolt.diy to quickly whip up prototypes or MVPs and charge for your time and creativity.
- SaaS Products: Got a niche idea? Build a Software as a Service app — like a workout tracker or a freelance invoice generator. Offer it on a subscription model and let it earn while you sleep.
- Template Sales: Create reusable templates for small business apps — think restaurant menus, booking systems, or inventory trackers. Then sell them on marketplaces like Gumroad or CodeCanyon.
- Ad Revenue: Develop apps that attract regular users — maybe a free budgeting tool or a local event tracker. Monetize these apps using in-app advertisements from platforms like Google AdMob.
- Affiliate Marketing: Integrate affiliate links or products into your apps. For example, an app for travel planning could feature deals from Booking.com or Airbnb and earn you a commission.
Bolt.diy isn’t just a toy — it’s a fully loaded toolbox that gives you the power to enter the developer economy without ever typing a single line of code.
2. MakeReels.ai: Create Engaging Reels with AI
checkout: https://makereels.ai/?via=abhishek-ashtekar
If you’re not already creating short-form videos, you’re leaving money on the table. MakeReels.ai is an AI-powered tool that turns your written content into polished video reels — complete with voiceovers, captions, and transitions. It’s ideal for creators, coaches, entrepreneurs, and really anyone looking to reach audiences on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube Shorts.
This tool simplifies the entire process. No need to mess with video editing software. Just plug in your script or blog post, choose a style, and let the AI do the heavy lifting.
5 Ways to Make Money with MakeReels.ai:
- Social Media Management: Offer services to small businesses or influencers who need consistent, engaging video content. You can create 10–15 reels per week and charge monthly retainers.
- YouTube Shorts: Launch your own channel focused on quick how-tos, inspirational quotes, or niche advice. Monetize through YouTube’s Partner Program and brand sponsorships.
- Affiliate Marketing: Use your reels to promote affiliate products. Add your unique link in the bio or description, and earn a commission for every sale or sign-up.
- Online Courses: Bundle a few related reels into micro-courses and sell them on sites like Gumroad, Teachable, or Udemy. Short-form educational content is trending right now.
- Brand Collaborations: Reach out to brands directly and offer to create custom promotional reels. Companies love influencers who can deliver high-quality content consistently.
The attention span of online audiences is shrinking, and MakeReels.ai helps you cater to that demand with zero editing stress. You can literally go from idea to reel in under 10 minutes — and get paid for it.

checkout: https://suno.com/home
Ever dreamed of being a music producer without investing in studio gear or learning complicated software? Suno AI lets you do just that. It’s an AI-driven tool that creates lo-fi beats and ambient music at the click of a button. Whether you’re a YouTuber, gamer, or someone who just loves creating chill vibes — this tool’s for you.
The output is surprisingly good. It’s not just generic background noise. We’re talking playlist-worthy tracks that sound like they came out of a cozy downtown café.
5 Ways to Make Money with Suno AI:
- YouTube Channel: Start a lo-fi channel and upload your AI-generated tracks. These types of channels consistently rake in ad revenue from millions of listeners who use lo-fi beats to study, relax, or sleep.
- Streaming Platforms: Distribute your music on Spotify, Apple Music, and Amazon Music using services like DistroKid or TuneCore. Earn royalties every time someone hits play.
- Sell Beats: Market your unique lo-fi beats to indie artists, rappers, or video creators. You can list them on platforms like BeatStars or even Fiverr.
- Background Scores: Offer custom background music for YouTubers, game developers, or app creators. You can charge a premium for exclusive tracks.
- Merchandising: Once your brand grows, design merch like hoodies, vinyl records, or stickers inspired by your music and sell them online.
Suno AI lets you enter the music industry without playing a single instrument. All you need is taste, a little curation, and the drive to build an audience.
4. Video2X: Upscale Videos Using AI
checkout: https://github.com/k4yt3x/video2x
Have a low-res video that’s almost unusable? Or maybe old family footage that needs a modern touch? Video2X is an open-source AI tool that can upscale videos with shocking clarity. It’s particularly useful for content creators who want to improve the quality of their video archives or freelancers looking to offer specialized editing services.
Unlike most free upscalers that leave a watermark or crush your quality, Video2X gives you full control and serious output. It’s a must-have if you dabble in video content.
5 Ways to Make Money with Video2X:
- Video Restoration Services: Offer to enhance old or low-quality videos for clients — especially YouTubers, archivists, or event videographers. People pay good money for high-quality memories.
- Stock Footage Sales: Take free or low-res footage, upscale it, and sell it as high-quality stock on sites like Shutterstock or Pond5.
- YouTube Content: Make your own high-quality videos, which can lead to better engagement, more subscribers, and higher ad revenue.
- Freelance Editing: Add video upscaling to your list of services on Fiverr or Freelancer. It’s a niche skill that not many offer.
- Online Courses: Teach others how to use Video2X in a step-by-step tutorial format. Sell it or post on YouTube to grow your audience.
Whether you’re enhancing home videos or prepping cinematic edits for a client, Video2X gives you the power of a studio in your living room.

Lovable.dev puts the power of tech startups in the hands of everyday dreamers. Whether you’re launching your first app or your fiftieth, it’s got the speed and simplicity you need.

But here’s the real goldmine: Firebase is fully compatible with many AI-powered tools. That means you can create smart, responsive, scalable apps that feel premium, even if you’re a solo creator just getting started.
5 Ways to Make Money with Google Firebase Studio:
- Freelance App Development: Many startups and small businesses want apps but can’t afford big development firms. You can build fast, scalable apps using Firebase and offer your services on platforms like Upwork or Freelancer.
- Subscription-Based Apps: Think habit trackers, journal apps, or personal finance tools. You can build these using Firebase and implement subscription models to earn recurring revenue.
- Client-Based Agency Work: Partner with businesses to create custom applications, from appointment booking systems to internal dashboards. Offer maintenance services for additional monthly income.
- Build a SaaS MVP: Have an idea for the next big software solution? Use Firebase to build your minimum viable product (MVP). Once it’s validated, scale it and introduce paid plans.
- Monetized Apps on App Stores: Publish your Firebase-powered apps on the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Earn through in-app purchases, ads (via AdMob), or subscriptions.
Firebase is the closest thing to having Google itself back your app idea. It handles the backend stress, so you can focus on what matters: growing your product and making money.
7. Pinokio AI: One-Click Launcher for AI-Powered Apps
checkout: https://pinokio.computer/
Pinokio AI isn’t just another AI tool — it’s like having an entire AI control center on your laptop. It bundles multiple open-source AI applications (like Stable Diffusion, Whisper, and Wan 2.1) and lets you launch them with a single click. No complex setup, no server costs. Just install and go.
Think of it as your all-in-one toolkit for everything from AI art to voice transcription. The beauty is that it runs locally, so you don’t need to rely on the cloud — and it’s all free.
5 Ways to Make Money with Pinokio AI:
- AI Art Services: Use Stable Diffusion to create stunning artwork and sell it on Etsy, Redbubble, or your own site. You can even take custom commissions.
- Video Creation for Social Media: Generate unique short videos using Wan 2.1 and post them on Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube Shorts. Monetize through brand deals or ad revenue.
- Transcription Services: Whisper is an incredibly accurate transcription tool. Use it to transcribe podcasts, interviews, or meetings, and offer your services on Fiverr or TaskRabbit.
- Voiceover and Dubbing: Combine Whisper and voice generation tools to create content for YouTube narrations, audiobooks, or TikTok storytelling. It’s fast, scalable, and lucrative.
- Local Freelancing Agency: Start a micro-agency using Pinokio AI to offer creative services — everything from content writing to video production — all powered by AI. Market to local businesses or online.
Pinokio AI gives you access to a studio-level suite of tools with zero overhead. It’s perfect for creators who want complete control and maximum profit.
